What Is Voice Republic
Voice Republic is the platform for the spoken word. We make it easy to live-stream your event, presentation, discussion or lecture—and keep it in our archive for those who missed it live.

Users from anywhere in the world can tune in and add their thoughts to the "comments" feed—in realtime. Right after the live broadcast, the recording is automatically saved in our archive. Anyone can access it on our platform at any time, or subscribe to it as a podcast.

If you already have recorded content from past events, you can easily upload files to Voice Republic. Starting at 12 euros per talk, you can boost your reach and position your content from the fields of politics, science, arts and culture, technology and business in a guaranteed high-quality environment.

Listening is free for all users.
