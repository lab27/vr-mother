Publish your own content online.

On Voice Republic, your content is in good company. In our audio archive you will find high-class lectures, discussions and readings from the fields of culture, sciences, politics and business. Whether via audio live stream or by uploading audio files: Reach an interested audience and increase your reach. Thanks to the browser-based technology of Voice Republic you stay flexible and do not require additional equipment. Starting at 12 euros per Talk, you can easily create your own audio channel.

The Voice Republic Live stream

Are you organizing a conference, discussion panel, or holding lectures and workshops yourself? With Voice Republic you can publish your content to the whole world and reach a large audience.

Broadcasting your own live stream is easy with the browser-based technology of Voice Republic. You create a talk in your Voice Republic account and during the event you click on "Start" and "Stop". The technology you need on site is only a computer with Internet access and an audio source. The comment feature gives you the opportunity to interact with your online audience. Listeners can comment and ask questions in real time. When your event is over, your audio content is in good hands with us. All talks are stored on the Voice Republic servers and are searchable in the archive immediately after the live broadcast.

The Voice Republic audio upload

If you do not want to stream live, but have already existing recordings, you can also use our audio archive. Just upload your audio files and add your own photos, descriptive texts and, if you want to, presentation slides. Everyone can now listen to your content online or download it as a podcast. You can easily embed the Voice Republic Player into your own web page and share the talks in social networks.

What Is Voice Republic
Voice Republic is the platform for the spoken word. We make it easy to live-stream your event, presentation, discussion or lecture—and keep it in our archive for those who missed it live.

Users from anywhere in the world can tune in and add their thoughts to the "comments" feed—in realtime. Right after the live broadcast, the recording is automatically saved in our archive. Anyone can access it on our platform at any time, or subscribe to it as a podcast.

If you already have recorded content from past events, you can easily upload files to Voice Republic. Starting at 12 euros per talk, you can boost your reach and position your content from the fields of politics, science, arts and culture, technology and business in a guaranteed high-quality environment.

Listening is free for all users.
